About Me

I am a results-driven Materials Scientist and Process Engineer with a Ph.D. in Materials Science and over 5 years of post-graduate expertise specializing in advanced crystallographic and structural characterization of complex systems, nuclear/fusion energy materials, and thin-film engineering.

My professional track record includes translating fundamental experimental data into scalable, reproducible workflows for industrial partners and national laboratory networks. Additionally, I bring deep leadership experience in laboratory safety coordination, ensuring zero-incident operations while managing complex chemical and hazardous environments.
