Professional Profile

Results-driven Materials Scientist and Process Engineer with a Ph.D. in Chemistry and extensive expertise in thin-film deposition, advanced structural characterization, and ultra-high vacuum (UHV) systems. Demonstrated success in executing high-stakes research milestones for multi-million dollar ARPA-E energy projects, engineering advanced ceramic coatings, and designing custom laboratory instrumentation. Adept at troubleshooting complex vacuum hardware, implementing Design of Experiments (DoE), and collaborating across multidisciplinary boundaries to translate fundamental materials science into robust, reproducible technical processes.
